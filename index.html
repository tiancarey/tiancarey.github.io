Apply

We welcome enquiries from motivated researchers interested in two-dimensional materials, composites, printed and wearable electronics, sensors and scalable nanomaterial processing.

PhD Opportunities

Prospective PhD researchers may join the group through funded studentships or by applying for an external postgraduate scholarship. Relevant funding routes may include Research Ireland postgraduate scholarships, university-funded studentships and international scholarship programmes.

If contacting the group, please prepare:

  • Your proposed funding route and expected application deadline
  • A sentence on why you want to join this group specifically
  • A sentence your research interests
  • Details of laboratory, device-fabrication experience or publications are a bonus but not necessary to start a PhD

Please note that I receive several emails a day. I try to reply to every candidate if you have provided the above; however, if I missed your initial enquiry, I apologise. Please email me again, i might have missed it. The shorter the email the better.

Postdoctoral Opportunities

Researchers interested in joining the group as postdoctoral fellows are encouraged to contact me well in advance of the relevant funding deadline. I can support top candidates in developing external fellowship proposals.

Potential fellowship routes include:

  • Marie Skłodowska-Curie Postdoctoral Fellowships
  • Research Ireland postdoctoral programmes
  • European Research Council and Horizon Europe projects
  • Relevant international mobility and national fellowship schemes

Postdoctoral applicants should normally provide the 3-4 sentences above that are requested for PhD students. Including a clear explanation of how the proposed work complements the group's expertise and how the group can also help your research.
